About Us

Kaloor's Favourite Football Venue

Playmakers Soccer Field is the go-to destination for football lovers in Kochi. Located conveniently near Kaloor International Stadium, our state-of-the-art facility offers a premium experience for players of all skill levels.

Whether you're organising a weekend league, a corporate match, or a casual kick-about with friends, Playmakers provides the perfect pitch, the best facilities, and an atmosphere that fuels your passion for the game.

  • ✓ FIFA-grade synthetic turf surface
  • ✓ Floodlit for evening & night matches
  • ✓ 5-a-side football & box cricket
  • ✓ Professional changing rooms & showers
  • ✓ Ample secure parking
  • ✓ On-site gear store
